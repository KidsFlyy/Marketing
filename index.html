Welcome To KidsFlyy!

As a parent, it's next to impossible to fly if you have multiple kids. Holding a baby while trying to carry your own luggage, the baby's luggage, the toddler's luggage, the carseat, the stroller, etc. is physically impossible. KidsFly alerts airport staff when you arrive to have an airport employee ready at the curb when you arrive.

Let's Go!

As a parent flying with young children can be a challenge, has this ever happened to you? You arrive either late or when you only have a few mintues to get to the gate, and you now have to juggle a million things.

You are thinking there is no way you are making this flight and you just want to give up.

Help Arrives

With KidsFly you can now set up your trip and alert airport staff you are on the way. Then when you get to the airport instead of worrying about everything you just worry about your kids. The airport staff will be there to basically give you an extra hand.

Pick Your Destination

KidsFly allows to choose any airline in all major cities. If you decide you don't need the help, no worries you can cancel the trip all together and not worry about any cancellation fees.
